Féileacán Cú and other characters from Metamorphosis Lost have made it to Asayoru Maid Cafe. Join us for a weekend with a specially themed custom menu and goodies to celebrate the birthday of Féileacán and Cú with Asayoru's magical maids.

Ticketed sessions that include meet & greets with Féileacán Cú will only be for Saturday, Nov. 8th.

During our collaboration, cafe sessions include menu items picked by Féileacán and Cú, along with special birthday freebies. Exclusive birthday and maid themed merchandise will also available to attendees for purchase on-site on all three (3) days (while supplies last). 

Purchase your meet & greet tickets or reserve your cafe seat on Asayoru Maid Cafe’s website <a href="https://www.asayorumaidcafe.com/event-details/birthday-butterflies-feileacan-cus-bday-party">here</a>!
